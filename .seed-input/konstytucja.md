# Personal Constitution — template

> Replace this file with your own constitution. The text below is a placeholder
> structure to show the format. Whatever you put here will be loaded as the
> initial content when you first open the Constitution screen.

## Roles

- Who am I in the most important relationships of my life?
- What does it mean to do well in each of those roles?

## Principles

- The non-negotiables. The things you will not trade.

## Aspirations

- Where am I going? What does the long-game look like?

## Anti-patterns

- The behaviors I want to catch myself doing — and stop.

---

This file is intentionally short. Your constitution should be a living document
you revisit during weekly and quarterly reviews, not something you write once
and forget. Edit it freely as you learn what matters most.
